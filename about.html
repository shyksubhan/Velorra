How We Started

Lahore to Everywhere

Founded in the heart of Lahore, Velorra started as a dream to bring world-class fashion to Pakistani wardrobes. We saw a gap — either mass-market clothing or imported luxury with no middle ground. Velorra fills that gap.

We partner with skilled local artisans and carefully selected international suppliers to bring you pieces that rival global brands — at prices that make sense for our market.

Every item in our collection passes through a rigorous curation process. We believe in quality over quantity, seasonless style over fast trends, and customer trust above all.
