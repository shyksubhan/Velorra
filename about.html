How We Started

Lahore to Everywhere

Founded in the heart of Lahore, Velorra started as a dream to bring world-class hair accessories to Pakistani women. We saw a gap — either cheap plastic accessories or overpriced imports with no middle ground. Velorra fills that gap.

We partner with skilled artisans and carefully selected suppliers to bring you pieces that make your hair look salon-ready — at prices that make sense for our market.

Every item in our collection passes through a rigorous curation process. We believe in quality over quantity, pieces that last beyond a season, and customer trust above all.
